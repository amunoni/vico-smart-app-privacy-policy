# Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

## Introduction
Vico Smart respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using the app.

## Information We Collect
The app may use SMS permissions to send and receive messages strictly for alarm control and emergency response functionality. No personal SMS messages are accessed.

## Use of Information
Information is used only to:
- Control alarm systems via SMS
- Send emergency alerts
- Improve app stability

## Data Sharing
We do not sell or share user data with third parties.

## Data Security
All data is stored locally on the user’s device and protected using standard security practices.

## User Control
Users can revoke permissions at any time through device settings.

## Children’s Privacy
This app is not intended for children under 13.
